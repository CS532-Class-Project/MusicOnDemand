Guangzhou is a national historical and cultural city and the radiation center of Cantonese culture. The China Import and Export Commodities Fair held every year attracts a large number of merchants, foreign-funded enterprises, and the world's top 500 companies. There is also a long history of food culture in Guangzhou, and many people call it "Better food in Guangzhou".

This is the most prosperous place in Xiguan. Within 1.5 kilometers, Taiping, Fengning (now Renmin Road), Dade, Yide, Huifu, Daxin, Huiai (now Zhongshan Sixth Road) in Donghan City The road section is connected to Shisanhang, Xidi, and Shamian in the south, connected to the "Three Treasures" (Baohua, Baoyuan, and Duobao Road) of Xiguan in the west, connected to rich townships such as Enzhou and Pantang in the north, and is close to Hualin Ancient Temple. morning tea 1

People in Guangzhou have morning tea, sometimes as breakfast, and usually the whole family sits around a table to share the happiness of family. Some go to work after drinking morning tea, while others use it for leisure. Most of the leisure people are retired old people in the neighborhood. They are generally the earliest to come and the latest to leave. They can sit in the teahouse from the morning when the teahouse opens until the morning tea "closes". This kind of tea drinkers do not go to luxury restaurants, high-end teahouses or hotels, but to economical small teahouses on the nearby streets. For example, if you go to a high-end tea house, the per capita consumption can be similar to that of a banquet. "Morning tea" is also a common way for Guangzhou people to socialize.

Yue Xiu Park

Yuexiu Park is the largest comprehensive park in Guangzhou. Yuexiu Mountain, the main body of Yuexiu Park, is named after Zhao Tuo, king of Nanyue in the Western Han Dynasty, who built the "Chaohan Terrace" on the mountain. There is a stone arch built in the Qing Dynasty in the garden, engraved with the four characters "Ancient Chu Pavilion", many historical books regard "Chu Ting" (or "Chu Pavilion") as the prototype of Guangzhou [1] . There are also Zhenhai Tower, ancient city wall, Sifang Fort, Sun Yat-sen Monument, Sun Yat-sen's Study and Management Office Stele, Wu Tingfang's Tomb, Ming Shaowu Junchen Tomb, Sailor's Pavilion, Wuyang Stone Statue, Wuyang Legend Sculpture Group, Spherical Water Tower and other scenic spots.
